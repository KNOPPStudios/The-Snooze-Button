1. Tossin' And Turnin' - Bobby Lewis

This one is kind of a no-brainer. It's one of only six songs from the entire decade of the 1960s to spend at least seven weeks at the top of the Pop chart. It was the Song Of The Year for 1961. With that kind of cred, "Tossin' And Turnin'" should be near the top of a list on a ton of different subjects. But on a list of songs about sleep, it's hard to make a case for anything else being in the #1 spot.
