    He's got listeners in more countries than he even knew existed. And for them, what John Watson is doing is making a huge difference.

    H

    John runs an online radio station that plays relaxation music and binaural beats to tens of thousands of people every month.

    We talk about how the project started, where it's headed and how musicians can get their material played online.

    Additionally, there's a quick clip from next week's guest, neuroscientist Dr. Guy Leschziner, about the science behind relaxation music and binaural beats.
